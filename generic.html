Voice Out Student Helpline Management System

One of the small scale project that i have worked on during my sophomore in college. I remember that my team decided to create this project as part of our requirements in my curriculum during the time of covid pandemic to provide a platform that can be used by the students to setup an appointment with the guidance counselor. In return, counselor can view their list of schedules which minimize and shorten the process. It involves a chat-based, and appointment scheduling features which enabled the user to interact with each other by providing a platform to counsel student during pandemic.

Through this project i have a learned a lot with regards to PHP and SQL language.
