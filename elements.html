Hi, I am Marvin Gregorio

I'm a self-taught, driven individual on the path to becoming a proficient web developer. My journey began in Olongapo City, Zambales, Philippines. I pursued a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) at Columban College, refining my foundational knowledge.

At 22 years old, I am resolutely set on establishing a thriving career in the realm of web development. My journey has encompassed various facets of this field, including a keen aptitude for UI/UX design. I adeptly utilize tools like Figma and Adobe Photoshop to bring a visually appealing dimension to my projects.

My proficiency extends across a diverse spectrum of languages critical to web development, including HTML, CSS, Javascript, Ajax, JQuery, PHP, SQL, and the robust Laravel framework. This comprehensive skill set empowers me to tackle multifaceted projects with confidence and creativity. Moreover, I'm enthusiastically delving into frameworks to further enhance my abilities in crafting innovative and functional websites.

Currently, I'm actively contributing as a part-time professional and remote freelancer. This hands-on experience has deepened my understanding of real-world development scenarios and honed my problem-solving skills. I'm fully primed to navigate the dynamic landscape of web development with a strong foundation, a fervent drive, and a solid belief in my capabilities.
